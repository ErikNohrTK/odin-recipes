Lasagna

Description

A recipe for italian lasagna. Healthy carb-loading done easy, with pasta, tomatoes and mince.

Ingredients
  • pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mince meat
  • Flour
  • Butter

Steps
  1. Fry the meat
  2. Prepare oven-safe dish
  3. Prepare the beschamel
  4. Place layers of meat and sauce separated by pasta plates
  5. Cook
